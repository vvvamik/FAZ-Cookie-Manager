## Legal bases (Art. 7 LGPD)

For **strictly necessary** cookies: legitimate interest (Art. 7, IX) and contractual/technical obligation (Art. 7, V).

For **all other** categories: **explicit consent** of the data subject (Art. 7, I), collected via the cookie banner before any non-essential cookie is set.

You may withdraw consent at any time (Art. 8, § 5) via the "Cookie settings" link.

We do **not** process sensitive personal data (Art. 5, II) via cookies without specific and highlighted consent (Art. 11 LGPD).
